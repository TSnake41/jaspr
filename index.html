JaspR - Just Another Software to Pull Rubbish

"JaspR, what's that ?"

JaspR is a software which cleans useless data from your system and your browsers to free disk space.

It comes with a pretty user interface, to make it even easier for anybody to release a hard drive of amounts of unnecessary files.

"What can I do with it ?"

JaspR will enable you to :

  • Clean Temporary Files
  • Clean Log Files
  • Empty Trash
  • Clean Thumb Cache
  • Clean Recent Files List
  • Clean Old Prefetch's Files
  • Deactivate Hibernation
  • Clean Cache (Firefox, Chrome, Edge, Internet Explorer, Safari and Opera)
  • Clean Cookies (Firefox, Chrome, Edge, Internet Explorer, Safari and Opera)
  • Clean History (Firefox, Chrome, Edge, Internet Explorer, Safari and Opera)

"How do I get it ?"

You will find JaspR here, on GitHub, but also on Sourceforge.

You will find the JaspR setup (executable file), download it and launch it. Follow the steps to install it, and "TAA-DAM", JaspR is now on your computer ! Extra-easy, isn't it ?

There's also a forum topic of JaspR on batch.xoo.it, I really recommend you to check this out if you are interessed.

"Alright, JaspR is installed, now how do I use it ?"

After the installation, you should find two shortcuts named "JaspR (x86)" and "JaspR (x64)" on your desktop. Launch the one which correspond to your processor's architecture. If you have a 32 bits version of Windows, then launch "JaspR (x86)". Else, if you have a 64 bits version of Windows, launch "JaspR (x64)".

Two windows appear on your screen :

This is the main menu window. Here, you just have to select which items JaspR will clean for you, and choose if you want to analyze the amount of data that you could save, or clean directly it.

This is the other window. It's a console in which you will see the process progress.
